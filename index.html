John Rickenbach started Rickenbach Construction Company in Astoria in 1965. For over the past 50 years, Rickenbach Construction has worked to become one of the Pacific North Coast’s foremost commercial and residential General Contractors. Jared Rickenbach, President, Diane Rickenbach, Vice-president, John Rickenbach, Secretary and Michelle Dieffenbach, Treasurer, have established the company’s reputation for honesty, dependability, teamwork, problem solving, quality craftsmanship, and schedule and project management.
