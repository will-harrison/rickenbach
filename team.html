Rickenbach Construction

Founders: John & Diane Rickenbach

John & Diane started Rickenbach Construction Company in Astoria in 1965. In 1989 Rickenbach Construction incorporated and has worked to become one of the Pacific North Coast’s foremost commercial General Contractors. They established the company’s reputation for honesty, dependability, teamwork, problem solving, quality craftsmanship, and schedule and project management.

President: Jared Rickenbach

Jared received a Construction Engineering Management degree with a minor in Business Administration from Oregon State University in 1999. While attending college he worked as a foreman for Rickenbach Construction on various projects during the summers. Jared joined the company full time in 1999 as construction foreman and became President - Project Manager in 2008and has been involved in all projects since that time. He is a Rotary International member, City of Astoria Gateway District Design Review Committee member and is active in NCBIA Local Builders Association.

Architect & Treasurer Michelle Dieffenbach

Michelle earned her Bachelor of Architecture degree in 1989 at the University of Oregon. She is also licensed in the State of California. Michelle joined the company in 1997 as an Architect to provide Design Build services and has served as Corporate Treasurer since 2008. Michelle serves on the City of Astoria Historic Landmark Committee.
